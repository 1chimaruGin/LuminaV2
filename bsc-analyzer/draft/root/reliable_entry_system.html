📡 Observable Signals at Entry — What Each Tells You

KOL Count (live, grows over time)
The single most powerful signal. Don't treat it as fixed at entry — monitor it in real-time and upgrade your position as each new KOL joins.

Dev Sell Amount (observable at entry)
Not a simple good/bad signal. Dev sell = 0 just means the token is very new. Large dev dumps into early pump actually mean the token survived that pressure.
dsell=0 → very fresh, unclear dsell <$500 → small exit, neutral dsell >$5k → heavy dump, avoid
⏱️
Token Age at Entry (age_blocks)
Fresh tokens (<100 blocks, <5 min old) have 22% TP1 hit rate. Old tokens (>1000 blocks) drop to 11%. Momentum decays fast in meme coins.
≤50 blocks (<2.5min) → best 50–500 blocks → medium >1000 blocks (>50min) → avoid
📊
Entry Mcap (current price at your entry)
Counter-intuitive: entry >$25k shows higher avg_x because those tokens already have momentum. Entry <$8k has low 2x hit rate — price bounces around too much early.

<5 blocks → likely coordinated → higher kc coming >500 blocks → independent → less predictable

🔑 Architectural Takeaways
✅ Treat kol_count as a live, growing signal. Don't enter a fixed position at slot_2. Enter small (Mode 1), then add more BNB each time a new KOL joins (Mode 2 → Mode 3). Your position grows as conviction grows.
⚠️ The 83% "never hit TP1" problem disappears at kc=3+. At kc=3, 39% hit 2x. At kc=4, ~45%. The old system's flaw was treating all 255 trades equally — 60% of them were fundamentally unworthy of a full position.
📊 Holder count is your most important missing filter. It confirms that real buyers exist beyond the KOL group. A token with 5 KOL buys and only 8 holders = the KOLs ARE the market. A token with 5 KOLs and 80 holders = genuine organic interest.
🔥 Dev sell is nuanced, not binary. Dev sell = 0 just means the token is fresh — not necessarily safer. What matters is the rate: dev_sell_usd / age_blocks. A dev who sold $3k over 10,000 blocks is less dangerous than one who sold $500 in 50 blocks.
✅ Block delta between KOL1 and KOL2 is a free signal you already have. Calculate it from kol_buys[1].buy_block - kol_buys[0].buy_block. Fast buys (<5 blocks) = coordinated group → expect more KOLs. Add this to your scoring immediately.
🚨 Graduated tokens kill your upside. The $58.8k graduation cap means any token that already graduated at entry gives you zero upside multiple. Auto-exclude or reduce to 0.01 BNB probe only if current_mcap ≥ $55k.
